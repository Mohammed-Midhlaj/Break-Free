Myths vs. Facts

Myth: "Recreational drug use is harmless."

Fact: Even occasional use can lead to addiction, health issues, and impaired judgment.

Myth: "Prescription drugs are always safe."

Fact: Misusing prescription drugs can be as dangerous as illegal drugs, causing overdose or dependency.

Myth: "I can stop anytime."

Fact: Addiction alters brain chemistry, making quitting extremely difficult without professional help.

Myth: "Drugs enhance creativity."

Fact: Drugs impair cognitive function and can stifle true creativity over time.
